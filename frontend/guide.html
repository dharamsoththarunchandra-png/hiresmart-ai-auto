Resume Screening Guide

A 5-step intelligent process to screen hundreds of resumes efficiently and eliminate human bias.

1

Upload & Input

Upload resumes (PDF, DOCX) in batch and enter detailed job requirements. Support for multiple file formats and bulk processing.

2

NLP Extraction

Advanced NLP techniques extract and parse resume information: skills, experience, education, certifications, and other key details.

3

Compute Match Score

AI-based APIs analyze resume data against job requirements and compute intelligent match scores based on skill alignment and qualification fit.

4

Smart Classification

Resumes are automatically classified as Suitable, Partially Suitable, or Not Suitable based on match scores and job requirements.

5

Review & Shortlist

View ranked candidates with detailed analysis. Filters reduce human bias and help recruiters quickly shortlist the most relevant candidates.
